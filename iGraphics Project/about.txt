The players must input their names before starting the game.There will be two separated 
dices and coins for two players.The first move of the coin will be done if the value of dice 
is 1.

When the dice is clicked, the coin will move according to the gained value of the dice. 
If the coin  lands at the bottom of a  ladder, it will move up to the top of the  ladder.
If the coin lands on the head of a  snake, it will slide down to the bottom of the  snake.

If anyone's coin of the two players will reach the 100 number,He'll be declared as the 
winner and  the game will be over.

And the ranking will be created with top five players who have won the game in 
minimum moves.Moves are counted through dice clicks.

Game can also be quit anytime in anywhere pressing "END" key.
